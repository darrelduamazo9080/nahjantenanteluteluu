14+ Poem On Heal The Earth Background. Does not have to have anything to do with the virus, just a healing earth. Write about a healing earth, from all of human ravages.

At the frontline of this rage sacrifices are being made. Heal the earth fell short for me in its attempt to be an interactive story. More like this earth than thine.

Rising out of the earth, feeding upon the tree does not seem to feel any kind of pain because the bleeding bark seemed to heal all the time.

Planet earth, my home, my placea capricious anomaly in the sea of spaceplanet earth are you justfloating by, a cloud of dusta minor globe, about to busta. And there are so many experts to choose from. It takes a unique person to notice. I can make myself invisible or small.
